Videography Website

Concept Site For College Project

This site design came from a college project where I had to redesign a real world website. I chose the videography company that were going to record our wedding day as they provide an excellent service and as we were in full wedding planning mode it was easy to empathise with other couples that would be looking for videography services.

I designed the site so that it would give you a WOW! moment when the homepage loaded with the beautiful, panoramic photo of a couple on the coast. The video gallery also has an aspirational image in the hero area. It generates emotion for viewers and they would engage with the content below. The concept behind the video player and the layout was for, upon development, the videos to load in the player area without a page reload using AJAX.
