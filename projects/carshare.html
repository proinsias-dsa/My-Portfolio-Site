CarShare Website

College Project

CarShare is a fictional company that allows users to share the cost of travel by splitting the fare and riding together. As part of this project I had to form a new team to scope and design the website which needed to be created using Bootstrap. With limited information on the company we had to form the brand and message that they would want to convey to their target audience.

I managed the project and assigned tasks to team members. Through brainstorming sessions and creative collaboration we identified that CarShare is a modern, economical and environmentally conscious company that wanted to bring people together. This formed the basis of our design. The logo needed to be modern and display a symbol of connection. With this in mind I drew CarShare's "CS" fused in an infinity like symbol to represent the connection of users and CarShare's connection with the environment.

The site layout is designed to demonstrate what CarShare is to new users and to offer easy sign up. The User Reviews offer social reinforcement to visitors and the custom drawn images, used for explaining how CarShare works, easily explain the process and benefits of using the service.
