NutriSaurus App Design

User Interface and User Experience

NutriSaurus is an app designed to enable users take the hassle out of maintaining a healthy daily diet. From selecting the right food to suit their dietary needs, tracking their progress towards that diet and then making hassle free, informed shopping decisions, NutriSaurus is designed to meet the needs of a wide range of users focused on maintaining a healthy diet.

The app was born out of a college project to create a digital product that would solve a health related problem faced by the public. We, as a team, identified the needs of users and created features that would help resolve those needs. Working in an Agile environment with a team based all over the country was a great experience which allowed me to become accustomed to remote collaboration. Using various communication tools, the ideal workflow ended up being Slack and Google Hangouts.

In all, there were 21 screens designed for the app. All of which have custom made icons or artwork. I also designed and created the logo to represent the NutriSaurus brand.
